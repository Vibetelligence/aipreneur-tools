Getting Started Tips

Whatever tool you choose, these tips will help you get the best results:

  • Start with clear prompts: The more specific your instructions, the better the output. Include context about your audience, tone, and goals.
  • Always edit and fact-check: AI tools are assistants, not replacements. Always review and refine the content.
  • Train the tool on your brand: Use brand voice features to teach the AI your company's tone and style.
  • Use templates: Templates are optimized for specific content types‚Äîuse them as starting points.
  • Iterate and refine: Don't settle for the first draft. Ask for revisions to get closer to what you need.
