Complete AI-Powered Workflow

Here's how to combine these tools into a complete creative workflow:

From Photo Shoot to Client Delivery

  1. Pre-Shoot Planning (1 hour before)
    Use AI tools to create mood boards and shot lists. Generate reference images with Midjourney for client approval.
  2. During the Shoot (Session time)
    Use AI-assisted culling software to identify the best shots in real-time. Many tools now offer instant preview analysis.
  3. Initial Culling (1-2 hours after)
    Use AI culling tools like AfterShoot or FilterPixel to automatically select the best images based on composition, focus, and expressions.
  4. Base Editing (2-4 hours)
    Apply AI-powered presets for base edits. Tools like Luminar AI or Lightroom AI Enhancements can batch-process your entire gallery.
  5. Retouching (1-3 hours)
    Use AI for skin smoothing, object removal, and background cleanup. Descript can also help create video slideshows with music.
  6. Client Delivery (Automated)
    Deliver via HoneyBook or similar platform with auto-organized galleries and download options.
  7. Marketing Automation (Ongoing)
    AI generates social posts from your shoot. Later schedules them automatically. ConvertKit sends a newsletter featuring new work.
