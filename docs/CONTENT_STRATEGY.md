## Strategy

### Content Pillars

Our content is organized around five core pillars that address different stages of the buyer's journey:

#### Pillar 1: Tool Reviews (40% of Content)

Deep-dive reviews of individual AI tools covering:

- Features and capabilities
- Pricing and value proposition
- Pros and cons based on actual testing
- Use cases for small businesses
- Alternatives and comparisons
- Final verdict and recommendations

**Example Topics:**
- Jasper AI Review: Is It Worth It for Small Businesses?
- Copy.ai vs Jasper: Complete Comparison 2026
- Notion AI: Full Review for Teams
- Descript Review: Video Editing for Beginners

#### Pillar 2: Comparisons (25% of Content)

Side-by-side tool comparisons helping readers make decisions:

- Feature-by-feature comparisons
- Pricing comparisons
- Use-case specific matchups
- Best tool for specific audiences
- Old vs new versions

**Example Topics:**
- Jasper vs Copy.ai vs Writesonic: Which Is Best?
- Notion vs ClickUp: Which Project Management Tool Wins?
- ChatGPT Plus vs Claude Pro: AI Assistant Comparison
- Best AI Writing Tools for Small Business (2026)

#### Pillar 3: How-To Guides (20% of Content)

Practical implementation guides:

- Getting started tutorials
- Advanced feature guides
- Integration walkthroughs
- Workflow automation guides
- Best practices

**Example Topics:**
- How to Use AI for Content Creation: Complete Guide
- Automate Your Small Business with AI Tools
- How to Create AI-Powered Videos for Marketing
- AI Tools for Email Marketing: Setup Guide

#### Pillar 4: Roundups (10% of Content)

Curated lists of best tools for specific needs:

- Best AI tools for specific use cases
- Budget-friendly options
- Industry-specific recommendations
- Free vs paid comparisons

**Example Topics:**
- 15 Best AI Tools for Small Business in 2026
- Free AI Tools That Actually Work
- Best AI Tools for Photographers
- AI Tools for Solopreneurs on a Budget

#### Pillar 5: Case Studies (5% of Content)

Real-world success stories and results:

- Small business AI adoption stories
- Time and cost savings examples
- Workflow transformation case studies
- ROI analysis from AI implementation

**Example Topics:**
- How This Solopreneur Saved 10 Hours/Week with AI
- Case Study: $500 to $5K/Month with AI Tools
- Real Results: Small Businesses Using AI

## Content Improvement Process

### Content Audit Schedule

| Frequency | Activity | Output |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| Weekly | Check Google Analytics | Traffic trends |
| Monthly | Review top 10 articles | Update opportunities |
| Quarterly | Full content audit | Content refresh plan |
| Annually | Complete strategy review | Strategy adjustments |

### Content Refresh Criteria

Update content when:

- **Traffic decline:** >20% drop in 30 days
- **Ranking drop:** Position drop >5 spots
- **Outdated info:** Tools changed significantly
- **New competitors:** New high-ranking content
- **6+ months old:** Scheduled refresh

### Content Refresh Process

1. **Identify:** Use analytics to flag underperforming content
2. **Analyze:** Check what's working for competitors
3. **Research:** Find updated information and new angles
4. **Update:** Refresh statistics, add new sections
5. **Optimize:** Improve SEO elements
6. **Republish:** Update date and notify search engines

### Content Quality Standards

| Element | Standard |
|---------|----------|
| Word count | 1,500-3,000 words for reviews/guides |
| Headings | H2/H3 hierarchy, keyword-rich |
| Images | 3-5 relevant images with alt text |
| Internal links | 3-5 relevant internal links |
| External links | 2-3 authoritative external links |
| Readability | Grade 6-8 reading level |
| Updates | Every 6 months minimum |
