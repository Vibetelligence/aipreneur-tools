## Priority Matrix

All article ideas are scored using our High Traffic + High Commission matrix:

| Priority | Traffic Score | Commission Score | Total Score | Action |
|----------|---------------|-----------------|-------------|--------|
| **P1 - Critical** | High (8-10) | High (8-10) | 16-20 | Publish immediately |
| **P2 - High** | High (8-10) | Medium (5-7) | 13-15 | Publish this month |
| **P3 - Medium** | Medium (5-7) | Medium (5-7) | 10-12 | Schedule for next month |
| **P4 - Low** | Low (1-4) | Any | <10 | Defer to Q2+ |

### Scoring Criteria

| Factor | High (8-10) | Medium (5-7) | Low (1-4) |
|--------|-------------|--------------|-----------|
| Search Volume | 2,000+/mo | 500-2,000/mo | <500/mo |
| Competition | Low-Medium | Medium | High |
| Affiliate Potential | High-ticket ($50+) | Mid-tier ($20-50) | Low (<$20) |
| Intent | Commercial/Transactional | Commercial Investigation | Informational |

### Evergreen Content Characteristics

- **Definition:** Content that remains relevant year-round, no expiration
- **Examples:** Tool comparisons, how-to guides, best-of lists
- **Keyword Indicators:** "Best," "vs," "review," "tools for [profession]"
- **Refresh Cycle:** Every 6 months minimum

### Timely Content Characteristics

- **Definition:** Content tied to seasons, events, or current trends
- **Examples:** 2026 releases, seasonal deals, case studies
- **Keyword Indicators:** Year markers, seasonal terms, "new"
- **Refresh Cycle:** Monthly updates, then archive after relevance
