# AI Business Tools — Business Plan

## Vision
Build a profitable affiliate content site that generates passive income by helping small business owners discover and choose AI tools.

## Mission
Curate, compare, and recommend the best AI tools for small businesses through comprehensive, honest reviews and guides.

## Target Audience

### Primary: Solopreneurs & Small Business Owners
- Age: 25-50
- Tech comfort: Medium to high
- Pain points: Time management, overwhelm, limited budget
- Goals: Work less, earn more, automate

### Secondary: Creative Professionals
- Photographers, videographers, designers
- Looking for AI to speed up workflows
- Higher willingness to pay for quality tools

---

## Content Strategy

### Content Pillars
1. **Tool Reviews** — Deep dives on individual tools
2. **Comparisons** — Tool vs tool, feature by feature
3. **How-To Guides** — Practical implementation
4. **Roundups** — Best tools for specific use cases
5. **Case Studies** — Real results from AI adoption

### Article Cadence
- **Launch:** 10-15 foundation articles
- **Month 1-6:** 4-6 new articles/month
- **Month 6+:** 2-4 new articles/month (maintenance)

### SEO Strategy
- Target commercial-intent keywords ("best AI tools for business")
- Long-tail keywords ("AI tools for freelance photographers")
- Regular content updates for freshness
