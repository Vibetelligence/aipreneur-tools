## Decision #1: Niche Selection
**Date:** February 2, 2026

**Decision:** Focus on "AI Tools for Small Business" as primary niche

**Rationale:**
- High search volume + high commercial intent
- Growing market with new tools constantly launching
- Good affiliate commissions (20-30%)
- Audience matches user's existing business audience

**Alternatives Considered:**
- Photography AI tools (too narrow)
- General productivity (too broad)
- Event industry only (too specific)

**Status:** ✅ Approved

## Decision #3: Tech Stack
**Date:** February 2, 2026

**Decision:** Static HTML + Cloudflare Pages

**Rationale:**
- Free hosting (no recurring costs)
- Fast performance (CDN included)
- Simple to maintain (no CMS updates)
- Easy deployment from GitHub

**Alternatives Considered:**
- WordPress — More features, but requires maintenance
- Squarespace — Monthly fees, less control

**Status:** ✅ Approved

---

## Decision #4: Affiliate Priority Order
**Date:** February 2, 2026

**Decision:** Apply to programs in this order:
1. ClickUp (best cookie window, 180 days)
2. Notion (highest commission, 50%)
3. Copy.ai (easy approval, good commission)
4. ConvertKit (creator-focused, easy approval)

**Rationale:**
- ClickUp has the longest cookie window (180 days) = more conversions
- Notion has the highest commission (50% first year)
- Copy.ai and ConvertKit are easy approvals

**Status:** ✅ Approved

## Decision #5: Content Strategy
**Date:** February 2, 2026

**Decision:** Focus on comparison and review content first

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Tool comparisons
- 30% How-to guides
- 20% Reviews
- 10% News/opinions

**Rationale:**
- Comparison content converts well
- How-to guides capture informational keywords
- Reviews build trust

**Status:** ✅ Approved

---

## Decision #6: Revenue Goals
**Date:** February 2, 2026

**Decision:** Target conservative revenue goals
- Month 3: First commissions
- Month 6: $500/month
- Month 12: $2,000/month
- Year 2: $5,000+/month

**Rationale:**
- Realistic for solo operation
- Allows for learning curve
- Doesn't overestimate traffic growth

**Status:** ✅ Approved
