Sample Creative Workflow

AI-Assisted Photo Shoot to Delivery

  1. Pre-shoot: Use AI tools to create mood boards and shot lists
  2. During shoot: AI-assisted culling to identify best shots in real-time
  3. Editing: Apply AI presets for base edits, then refine manually
  4. Retouching: Use AI for skin smoothing, object removal
  5. Delivery: Auto-enhance and watermark images
  6. Marketing: AI generates social posts and email announcements
